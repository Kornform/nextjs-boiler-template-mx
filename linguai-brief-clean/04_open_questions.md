# Open Questions

These questions are intentionally unresolved.
They should remain open until the relevant phase requires a decision.

## Brand

- What is the final product name?
- Should the brand feel primarily friendly, premium, technical, or hybrid?
- Is there an existing visual identity to inherit later?

## Messaging

- What should the primary homepage promise be?
- How strongly should pricing/value comparisons be used?
- How visible should Pro be compared to Free?

## Information Architecture

- Which pages are needed first?
- Does the site start as a single-page landing page or multi-page website?
- How much technical detail belongs on the homepage vs subpages?

## Visual Direction

- Light, dark, or hybrid?
- Minimal, premium, editorial, or product-friendly?
- How product-led should the visual language be?

## Product Presentation

- Will real plugin screenshots be available?
- Should the design rely on mockups, illustrations, or simple UI framing?
- Which features deserve strongest prominence first?

## Commercial

- Is pricing final?
- Is there one Pro tier or multiple paid tiers?
- What is the exact download / trial / purchase flow?
